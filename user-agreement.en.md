## 1. Service Scope

The app provides a local family records management tool, including record creation, editing, deletion, search and filtering, maintenance status, record templates, and attachment reference management. The specific features are subject to the version you install and use.

## 2. Usage Rules

- You should ensure that the records you enter, save, and maintain come from lawful sources, contain accurate content, and comply with applicable laws and regulations.
- You must not use the app to save or distribute content that is illegal, infringes the lawful rights of others, harms cybersecurity, or disrupts public order.
- You are responsible for confirming the accuracy, completeness, and timeliness of your records.

## 3. Data and Backups

By default, the app stores records only on your device and does not provide cloud backup or cross-device recovery. Uninstalling the app, clearing app data, device failure, system restoration, or deleting records yourself may cause data loss. Please keep originals or backups of important materials yourself.

## 4. System Capabilities

When you use features such as "Add Attachment", the app may call the system file picker to select files. You decide whether to select a file. Canceling file selection does not affect saving records without attachments.

## 5. Responsibility Boundaries

The app does not judge the authenticity, validity, or professional conclusions of certificates, contracts, medical, insurance, financial, or other materials, and it does not replace legal, medical, insurance, or financial advice. You are responsible for decisions made based on your records.

When this agreement is updated, the app will prompt you to read and confirm it in an appropriate place. If you do not agree to this agreement or later changes, please stop using the app.
