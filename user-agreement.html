你应确保录入、保存和维护的资料来源合法、内容真实，并不得使用本应用保存或传播违法违规、侵犯他人权益的内容。

The app provides a local family records management tool, including record creation, editing, deletion, search and filtering, maintenance status, record templates, and attachment reference management.

2. Usage Rules

You should ensure that the records you enter, save, and maintain come from lawful sources and contain accurate content. You must not use the app to save or distribute content that is illegal, non-compliant, or infringes the rights of others.

3. Data and Backups

By default, the app stores records only on your device and does not provide cloud backup or cross-device recovery. Uninstalling the app, clearing app data, device failure, or system restoration may cause data loss. Please keep originals or backups of important materials yourself.

4. Responsibility Boundaries

The app does not judge the authenticity, validity, or professional conclusions of certificates, contracts, medical, insurance, financial, or other materials, and it does not replace legal, medical, insurance, or financial advice.

When the agreement is updated, the app will prompt you to read and confirm it again. If you do not agree to this agreement or later changes, please stop using the app.
