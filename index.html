隐私摘要

本应用是本地家庭资料管理工具。当前版本不提供账号体系、云同步、网络上传、广告、统计分析或第三方 SDK；你录入的资料和附件元数据默认保存在设备本地。只有当你主动点击“添加附件”时，应用才会调用系统文件选择器。

1. 个人信息处理清单

本应用仅处理你在本机主动录入或选择的信息。具体如下：

信息类型 处理场景 是否上传或共享
资料标题、分类、家庭成员名称、标签、日期、备注、维护状态 用于创建、展示、编辑、搜索、筛选和管理家庭资料记录 不上传，不向第三方共享
附件文件名称、类型、大小、本地引用 URI 仅在你主动添加附件后，用于在应用内展示附件列表和定位本地文件引用 不上传，不向第三方共享
你主动写入备注或资料中的其他内容 用于保存和展示你自行维护的资料内容 不上传，不向第三方共享

如果你自行录入证件、医疗、保险、财务、家庭成员等敏感内容，本应用只按你的操作在本机保存和展示，不会主动识别、上传或对外提供这些内容。请你根据需要谨慎录入并自行备份重要原件。

2. 使用目的

上述信息仅用于实现以下本地功能：

  • 资料建档、查看、编辑和删除。
  • 搜索筛选、到期状态、维护状态和资料模板展示。
  • 附件列表展示、本地引用管理和本地数据清理。

本应用不会将你的资料用于广告投放、画像分析、个性化推荐、商业营销或与本地资料管理无关的用途。

3. 权限和系统能力

  • 本应用不在启动时申请通讯录、定位、相机、麦克风、短信、电话等权限。
  • 只有你点击“添加附件”时，才会打开系统文件选择器。文件选择器由系统提供，你可以选择文件，也可以取消选择。
  • 你取消文件选择后，仍可保存无附件资料。
  • 如后续版本新增权限或第三方 SDK，我们会在应用内和本政策中同步说明处理目的、信息类型和控制方式。

4. 存储、共享和跨境

  • 资料和附件元数据保存在你的设备本地。
  • 本应用当前不提供账号、云同步、网络上传、广告、统计分析或第三方 SDK。
  • 本应用不向第三方出售、出租、共享或转让你的资料。
  • 本应用当前不进行个人信息跨境传输。

5. 你的权利

你可以通过应用内功能管理你的数据：

  • 查看、修改和删除单条资料。
  • 在“设置与数据”中清理本地资料和附件元数据。
  • 通过系统应用管理清理应用数据或卸载应用。

清理本地数据不会删除系统文件管理中的原始文件。如你对隐私政策或数据处理有疑问，可以通过本页底部邮箱联系我们。

6. 未成年人保护

本应用面向一般家庭资料管理场景，并非专门面向儿童。未成年人使用本应用前，应取得监护人同意并在监护人指导下录入和管理资料。监护人如发现未成年人录入了不适当信息，可以在设备本地删除相关资料或清理应用数据。

7. 保留期限与安全

资料会保留到你主动删除、清理应用数据或卸载应用为止。请妥善保管设备解锁凭据，避免他人未经授权访问你的设备，并自行备份重要原始文件。

8. 政策更新

当隐私政策发生重大变化，或应用新增涉及个人信息处理的能力时，我们会在应用内或页面中更新说明，并在需要时提示你重新阅读和确认。

Privacy Summary

This app is a local family records management tool. The current version does not provide accounts, cloud sync, network uploads, advertising, analytics, or third-party SDKs. Records and attachment metadata are stored locally on your device by default. The system file picker is opened only when you tap "Add Attachment".

1. Personal Information Processing List

The app only processes information that you actively enter or select on your own device:

Information Type Scenario Upload or Sharing
Record title, category, family member name, tags, date, notes, and maintenance status Used to create, display, edit, search, filter, and manage family records Not uploaded or shared with third parties
Attachment file name, type, size, and local reference URI Used only after you add an attachment, to display attachment lists and locate local file references in the app Not uploaded or shared with third parties
Other content you voluntarily enter in notes or records Used to save and display the records you maintain yourself Not uploaded or shared with third parties

If you voluntarily enter sensitive content such as certificates, medical, insurance, financial, or family member information, the app only stores and displays it locally according to your actions. The app does not actively identify, upload, or provide that content externally.

2. Purposes of Use

The information above is used only for these local features:

  • Create, view, edit, and delete records.
  • Display search filters, expiration status, maintenance status, and record templates.
  • Display attachment lists, manage local references, and clean local data.

The app does not use your records for advertising, profiling, personalized recommendations, marketing, or purposes unrelated to local record management.

3. Permissions and System Capabilities

  • The app does not request contacts, location, camera, microphone, SMS, phone, or similar permissions when it starts.
  • The system file picker opens only when you tap "Add Attachment". You may select a file or cancel the selection.
  • If you cancel file selection, you can still save a record without an attachment.
  • If future versions add permissions or third-party SDKs, we will explain the purpose, information type, and control method in the app and in this policy.

4. Storage, Sharing, and Cross-border Transfer

  • Records and attachment metadata are stored locally on your device.
  • The app currently does not provide accounts, cloud sync, network uploads, advertising, analytics, or third-party SDKs.
  • The app does not sell, rent, share, or transfer your records to third parties.
  • The app currently does not transfer personal information across borders.

5. Your Rights

You can manage your data through in-app features:

  • View, edit, and delete individual records.
  • Clean local records and attachment metadata in "Settings and Data".
  • Clear app data or uninstall the app through system app management.

Cleaning local data does not delete the original files in the system file manager. If you have questions about this policy or data processing, contact us through the email address at the bottom of this page.

6. Minors

The app is designed for general family records management and is not specifically directed at children. Minors should obtain guardian consent and use the app under guardian guidance. Guardians can delete relevant local records or clear app data if inappropriate information has been entered.

7. Retention and Security

Records are retained until you delete them, clear app data, or uninstall the app. Please keep your device unlock credentials secure, prevent unauthorized access to your device, and back up important original files yourself.

8. Policy Updates

If this policy changes materially, or if the app adds capabilities involving personal information processing, we will update the explanation in the app or on this page and prompt you to read and confirm it when necessary.
